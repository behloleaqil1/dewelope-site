Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dewelope (“Dewelope”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information from visitors and clients of the website https://dewelope.com (the “Site”). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — for example, when you fill in the contact form, request a proposal, or send us an email. This typically includes your name, email address, company name, and the contents of your message.
  • Information collected automatically — when you visit the Site, our hosting provider and analytics tooling may automatically collect data such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of access.
  • Information from cookies and similar technologies — see “Cookies and tracking” below.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries, proposals, and engagement requests.
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site.
  • Understand visitor behaviour in aggregate so we can improve content and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
  • Serve and measure advertisements (see “Advertising” below).

3. Cookies and tracking

The Site uses cookies and similar technologies (such as web beacons and local storage) to remember preferences, measure traffic, and support advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some parts of the Site may not function correctly if you do.

4. Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google, to serve ads when you visit the Site. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visits to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet. Google uses the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads based on prior visits.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • aboutads.info/choices (Digital Advertising Alliance)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

5. Third-party vendors

In addition to Google, we may use third-party services for analytics (e.g., privacy-respecting traffic measurement), email delivery (e.g., EmailJS for contact-form submissions), and infrastructure (e.g., DNS, hosting, content delivery). These vendors process information on our behalf and under their own privacy terms.

6. Data retention

We retain contact-form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and to maintain records of our engagement discussions, after which we delete or anonymise the data unless retention is required for legal or accounting reasons.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict our processing of your personal information, and to object to processing or to request data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@dewelope.com.

8. Children

The Site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. International users

Dewelope is based in Pakistan. If you access the Site from outside Pakistan, you understand that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Pakistan or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through a notice on the Site.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@dewelope.com or by post to Dewelope, Rawalpindi, Pakistan.
